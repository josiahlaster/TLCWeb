About Transitions Legacy Center

Building bridges to your future through meaningful peer connections

Our Mission

Transitions Legacy Center (TLC) delivers comprehensive, trauma informed peer support that bridges the gap between challenge and change. Rooted in lived experience, TLC empowers individuals to take control of their journey, build resilience, and create lasting independence through compassion, connection, and community.

Our Services

Our work follows the Sequential Intercept Model, providing support at key touchpoints where care and community can make the greatest impact. Whether it’s accessing housing, enrolling in school, finding a job, or simply feeling heard, TLC walks alongside each individual with compassion and consistency. At TLC, we do more than support transitions we build bridges to stability, relationships that heal, and legacies that empower. Every step forward becomes part of a larger story one written not by systems, but by the young people we serve.
